Intro

Taking a stroll through popular local stores and nurseries I came to realization that what I value most in plants - fragrance - is almost non existent in modern plant varieties. The hybrydization focused mostly on desease resistance and showy appearance neglected fragrance as valuable feature of a plant. I decided to turn my life long passion into business opportunity and open Fragrant Plants Nursery to offer the joy of fragrant plants back to our gardens and homes. I grow and sell a variety of plants

Please contact me to request current inventory. Or check out my offer

Any time of the year I can find for you or your loved one a treasure to cherish for years to come.

About

My name is Mila and I opened Fragrant Plants Nursery because of my life long lasting love for plants. I always loved growing and collecting beautiful plants but also reading and learing about them. I constantly study to widen my knowledge about botany, landscape design and environment. I live in Bothell, WA with my two wonderful children and awesome husband.
