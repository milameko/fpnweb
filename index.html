Intro

Taking a stroll through popular local stores and nurseries I came to realization that what I value most in plants - fragrance - is almost non existent in modern plant varieties. The hybrydization focused mostly on desease resistance and showy appearance neglected fragrance as valuable feature of a plant. I decided to turn my life long passion into business and open Fragrant Plants Nursery to offer the joy of fragrant plants back to our gardens and homes. I grow and sell a variety of plants

Please contact me to request current inventory. Or check out my offer.

Any time of the year I can find for you or your loved one a treasure to cherish for years to come.

About

I'm Mila, the proud owner of Fragrant Plants Nursery, where I get to combine my passion for plants, nature, and landscape design to bring beauty and serenity to our customers' outdoor spaces. When I'm not tending to our plants, you can find me spending time with my wonderful husband and our two amazing kids. We love exploring the great outdoors together, whether it's a family hike or a simple walk in the park. And when I need a break from all the greenery, I enjoy stand-up comedy shows and sit-coms. I'm so grateful to be able to share my love of nature and plants with our community, and I look forward to helping you find the perfect fragrant plants for your home!
